I am Master Student at the University of Munich.

My research focuses on the formation and evolution of our home galaxy, the Milky Way. Using a combination of analytic theory, observations, data visualization, and numerical simulations, I investigate the structure and dynamics of the local interstellar medium and examine how stars originate from their galactic environment.
