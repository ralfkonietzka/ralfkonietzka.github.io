About me

I am currently an ITC Fellow working at the Harvard-Smithsonian Center for Astrophysics. In my research, I aim to understand the cycle of star formation in galaxies. I use a mixture of analytic theory and numerical simulations to predict the observable signatures of star formation, as well as the properties of the molecular gas reservoirs in which stars are formed. By comparing theory to observations, I work to quantify the relative roles of galactic-scale physics (e.g. galactic-dynamical processes) and stellar feedback in setting the star formation rate and efficiency in different galaxy types.
